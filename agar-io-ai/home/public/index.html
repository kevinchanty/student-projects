Agar IO
Introduction of Agar IO
Agar.io is a multiplayer online game that imitate cells
where the player can gain size through eating foods in the environment and also prey on other players that are smaller than the player
Simplified version for AI training
Initially, we would like to train an AI to play the full game of Agar IO
However, the environment is too complicated that we do not have enough time to train our agent with all feature enabled
At last, we decided to simplify the game with smaller venue and eating food, gaining mass would be the only goal for the AI
